About Omnifood

We started this project in 2019, after covid striked we realize how many people don't have time or don't know how to properly cook at home. For all those people the fact that the restaurant were closed were such a complicated episode. This is how we had the idea of helping people eat better, fresher and cheaper without wasting any time.

Our goal

Eat well again

Thanks to Omnifood, you don't have to worry about anything. Once you select your diet preferences we deliver right to your doorstep freshly cooked meals.

Our Team

10 chefs just for you

We have a full team of chefs highly skilled for each type of diet, they also work as a team with dietetician and nutritionist to create for you the helthiest menu for the week

Our Principe

We also take care of the planet

In order to be as green as possible, all our ingredients are locally shopped. With Omnifood you get a meal for one person, no waste possible.
